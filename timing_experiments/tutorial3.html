Tips and Tricks

Oftentimes the document will be long and complex, and it may be ambiguous whether something is coreferent or not, or where the first mention of an entity lies. Here are some good tips for this:

  • Use ctrl-f. It will come in very handy.
  • Use the "predicted" coreferences highlighted on the text as hints.
  • If you truly don't know an answer, just make your best guess and move on. Remember, we just want the timing information.
